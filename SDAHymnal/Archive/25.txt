25  Praise the Lord, His Glories Show


1

Praise the Lord, His glories show, Alleluia! Saints within His courts below, Alleluia! Angels 'round His throne above, Alleluia! All that see and share His love, Alleluia!


2

Earth to heaven and heaven to earth, Alleluia! Tell His wonders, sing His worth, Alleluia! Age to age and shore to shore, Alleluia! Praise Him, praise Him evermore! Alleluia!


3

Praise the Lord, His mercies trace, Alleluia! Praise His providence and grace, Alleluia! All that He for man hath done, Alleluia!
All He sends us through His Son. Alleluia!
